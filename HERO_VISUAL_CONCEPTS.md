## üé¨ VIDEO SPECIFICATIONS

If creating video (recommended for premium feel):

### Technical Specs:
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (minimum) or 3840x2160 (4K preferred)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (standard) or 21:9 (cinematic wide)
- **Duration**: 10-15 seconds (seamless loop)
- **Frame Rate**: 24fps (cinematic) or 30fps (smooth)
- **File Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **File Size**: Under 10MB (compressed for web)

### Motion Guidelines:
- **Speed**: SLOW (creates gravitas, not rushed)
- **Movement**: Smooth, deliberate (no shaky cam)
- **Loop**: Seamless (end frame matches start frame)
- **Camera**: Slow dolly, rotation, or static
- **Focus**: Subtle shifts in depth of field okay

### Avoid in Video:
- ‚ùå Fast cuts or rapid motion (too flashy)
- ‚ùå Text overlays (keep for HTML overlay)
- ‚ùå Sound (will be muted anyway)
- ‚ùå Busy backgrounds (distracting)

## üñºÔ∏è STATIC IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS

If creating static image instead:

### Technical Specs:
- **Resolution**: 3840x2160 (4K) or higher
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **File Format**: JPG (web-optimized) or WebP
- **File Size**: Under 500KB (compressed)
- **Color Space**: sRGB (for web)

### Composition Guidelines:
- **Rule of Thirds**: Position key elements off-center
- **Depth**: Use shallow depth of field or layering
- **Negative Space**: Leave room for text overlay (headline + buttons)
- **Focal Point**: Clear area of interest (not too busy)

## ‚ùå AVOID THESE MISTAKES

### Visual Mistakes:
- ‚ùå Too busy/cluttered (distracts from message)
- ‚ùå Consumer-facing aesthetic (looks like phone ads)
- ‚ùå Overly futuristic/sci-fi (not credible)
- ‚ùå Warm color grading (orange/yellow - wrong brand)
- ‚ùå Low resolution (looks cheap)
- ‚ùå Generic stock photo vibes (overused images)

### Technical Mistakes:
- ‚ùå File too large (>10MB for video, >500KB for image)
- ‚ùå Wrong aspect ratio (must be 16:9)
- ‚ùå Video doesn't loop seamlessly (jarring)
- ‚ùå Video has sound (will be muted anyway, wasted generation)
- ‚ùå Not mobile optimized (test on phone!)

### Branding Mistakes:
- ‚ùå Too flashy/animated (George wants gravitas)
- ‚ùå Looks like tech startup (needs institutional feel)
- ‚ùå Doesn't represent sectors (semiconductors/robotics/AI/manufacturing)
- ‚ùå Too abstract (loses connection to industrial thesis)
