### Introduction

Silicon Century Capital ("SCC," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website or communicate with us regarding investment opportunities.

By using our website or providing us with your information, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

### Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

**Personal Information:**
- Name, email address, phone number, and company affiliation
- Investment interests and qualifications
- Accredited investor status and financial information (when applicable)
- Professional background and business information

**Technical Information:**
- IP address, browser type, and device information
- Website usage data and analytics
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

**Communications:**
- Email correspondence, form submissions, and inquiry details
- Meeting notes and discussion records

---

### How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

- **Investment Evaluation:** To assess potential investment opportunities and partnerships
- **Communications:** To respond to inquiries and share information about our investment strategy and portfolio companies
- **Regulatory Compliance:** To comply with SEC regulations, anti-money laundering requirements, and other legal obligations
- **Website Improvement:** To maintain, optimize, and enhance our website functionality
- **Business Operations:** To conduct due diligence and manage investor relationships

---

### Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

**Service Providers:**
We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist with website hosting, email communications, legal counsel, and other business operations. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

**Legal Requirements:**
We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, subpoena, or court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

**Business Partners:**
With your consent, we may share information with co-investors, portfolio companies, or other business partners in connection with specific investment opportunities.

**Business Transfers:**
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

---

### SEC and Regulatory Compliance

As a private investment firm, Silicon Century Capital complies with applicable securities laws and regulations, including:

- SEC Rule 506(c) of Regulation D
- Verification of accredited investor status when required
- Anti-money laundering (AML) and Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements

We may request documentation to verify your accredited investor status before providing access to certain investment materials or opportunities. All verifications are conducted in accordance with SEC regulations.

---

### Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

- Secure data storage and encryption
- Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
- Regular security assessments and updates

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. For confidential investment discussions, we recommend using secure communication channels.

### Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

**Access and Correction:**
You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us at privacy@siliconcenturycapital.com.

**Opt-Out:**
You may opt out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

**Data Deletion:**
You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements and ongoing business relationships.

**Cookie Management:**
You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

### Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Retention periods vary based on:

- The nature of the information

### International Data Transfers

Silicon Century Capital operates primarily in the United States. If you are accessing our website from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

Our website and services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will promptly delete it.
