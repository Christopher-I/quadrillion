## WEBSITE REQUIREMENTS

### Page Structure (~10 pages)
1. **Home** - Hero with video background
2. **About / Strategy** - Investment approach
3. **Investment Focus** - The "New Industrial Core"
4. **Industries** - Semiconductors, Robotics, Advanced Manufacturing
5. **Team** - Leadership bios
6. **Advisory Board** - Separate section
7. **Portfolio** - Structure ready for future companies
8. **Contact** - Dual form setup
9. **Privacy Policy**
10. **Terms of Use**

### Key Features

#### Dual Call-to-Actions (SEC Compliant)
1. **Investor CTA**: "Learn More" / "Become an Investment Partner" (cannot say "Invest With Us")
2. **Transaction CTA**: "Send Us a Deal" / "Propose a Transaction"

#### Visual Elements
- **Hero Video**: Background video (Pond5) - semiconductors/robotics
- **Logo Banner**: Semiconductor company logos for credibility
- **Industrial Imagery**: Authentic photos of manufacturing, chips, robotics
- **Minimal Animation**: Subtle hover states only - maintain gravitas

#### Technical Requirements
- **Two Separate Contact Forms**:
  - Investor inquiry form
  - Transaction/deal inquiry form
- **CMS Setup**: For future portfolio company additions
- **Mobile Responsive**: All devices
- **Basic SEO**: Meta descriptions, title tags, clean URLs
- **Performance**: Fast load times

## BRAND SYSTEM

### Design Philosophy: "Institutional Industrial"
**Aesthetic**: Institutional finance meets advanced manufacturing
**Tone**: Calm, credible, precise
**Approach**: Professional, modern, NOT flashy

### Color Palette
- **Deep Navy**: #294c89 (primary)
- **Teal**: #17b890 (accent)
- **Light Gray**: #dee5e5 (backgrounds)
- **Dark Green**: #082d0f (secondary accent)

### Typography
- **Headers**: Quixote
- **Body Copy**: Nanum Gothic
- **Hierarchy**: Clear structure for skim reading by institutional investors

### Core Design Principles
1. Authentic industrial imagery - semiconductors, robotics, manufacturing lines
2. Clarity and hierarchy - content structured for skim reading
3. Limited animation - subtle hover states and transitions to maintain gravitas
4. Consistent typography - Quixote + Nanum Gothic
5. Alternating light/dark backgrounds with strong whitespace

## CHRISTOPHER'S PROPOSAL RATIONALE

### Why Webflow Was Recommended
1. **Speed**: 3-week timeline achievable
2. **George's Familiarity**: Already knows the platform
3. **Quality**: Can achieve "Institutional Industrial" aesthetic
4. **CMS Independence**: George can manage updates himself
5. **Cost-Effective**: At $7,500, good balance of speed and quality

### Why NOT Squarespace
- More expensive ($10,500)
- Less capable
- Requires more custom code workarounds
- Harder to achieve desired aesthetic

### Custom Option (Not Chosen)
- Would have been $7,500, 4 weeks
- Best long-term value, full ownership
- But needed 1 extra week (couldn't accommodate)
